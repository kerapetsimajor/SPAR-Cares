Guidelines

Home Guidelines

Eligibility:

  • Non-profit organisations registered with the Department of Social Development (or equivalent) are eligible to apply.
  • Projects must align with at least one of the SPAR Cares pillars: Nutrition, Education, or Female Empowerment.
  • Projects should demonstrate a clear and measurable impact on the target community.
  • Priority will be given to projects addressing critical needs within the local communities SPAR serves.

Application Process:

  • Submit the completed Sponsorship Application Form online.
  • Ensure all required information is provided accurately and completely.
  • Incomplete applications may not be considered.
  • Supporting documentation (e.g., project budget, organisational registration certificate, beneficiary lists or confirmation of beneficiary groups, project plan) must be submitted as requested.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • The evaluation process will consider factors such as:
  • The project's alignment with SPAR Cares' mission and pillars.
  • The project's potential for significant and sustainable impact.
  • The organisation's track record and financial stability.
  • The clarity and feasibility of the project plan.
  • The organisation's capacity to effectively implement and manage the project.

Decision-Making:

  • The SPAR Cares team will review all applications and make funding decisions based on the evaluation criteria.
  • Applicants will be notified of the decision within 4-6 weeks of the application deadline.

Reporting:

  • Successful applicants must submit regular progress reports and a final impact report
  • SPAR Cares reserves the right to monitor the progress of funded projects.
